Site Plan: Discover Port Harcourt

W05 Project | WDD 231: Web Frontend Development

1. Site Name

Discover Port Harcourt – This name was chosen because it highlights the city’s culture, attractions, and business opportunities, encouraging visitors and businesses to learn more about the “Garden City.”

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as a digital hub for the Abuja Chamber of Commerce to promote Port Harcourt. It will provide information about tourist attractions, cultural activities, and economic opportunities. Visitors can learn about events, connect with businesses, and explore investment opportunities in the city.

3. Scenarios

  • What are the best tourist attractions to visit in Port Harcourt?
  • How can a business register with the Chamber of Commerce?
  • What events or festivals are happening in Port Harcourt this month?

4. Color Schema

The website will use a clean and professional palette:

  • Green (#16a34a): Buttons, highlights, and accents.
  • Navy Blue (#1e3a8a): Headers, navigation, and footer background.
  • Gray (#374151): Paragraph text and secondary details.

5. Typography

The font chosen is Inter for its modern and professional readability:

  • Headings: Inter Bold, navy blue.
  • Body Text: Inter Regular, dark gray.
  • Accents: Inter Semi-Bold, green highlights for emphasis.
