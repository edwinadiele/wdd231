NP Membership Benefits

This membership is specifically for non-profit organizations and comes with no annual fee. Benefits include:

  • Basic listing in the Chamber directory.
  • Access to Chamber newsletters and general communications.
  • Invitation to select Chamber events (some may require a separate fee).
  • Networking opportunities with other non-profits and businesses.

Bronze Membership Benefits

The Bronze Membership is ideal for small and growing businesses. Benefits include:

  • Standard listing in the Chamber directory.
  • Access to all Chamber newsletters and communications.
  • Discounts on Chamber events and workshops (e.g., 10% off).
  • Opportunity to attend exclusive "New Member Welcome" events.
  • Basic business advocacy support.

Silver Membership Benefits

The Silver Membership offers enhanced benefits for established businesses looking for more visibility. Benefits include:

  • Enhanced listing in the Chamber directory with logo.
  • Priority access to networking events and business mixers.
  • Increased discounts on Chamber events and training (e.g., 25% off).
  • Opportunity to host or co-host a Chamber event.
  • Inclusion in a quarterly "Member Spotlight" feature in the newsletter.
  • Access to a members-only resource library.

Gold Membership Benefits

The Gold Membership provides premium benefits and maximum exposure for leading organizations. Benefits include:

  • Premium, top-tier listing in the Chamber directory with prominent logo and description.
  • Complimentary access to all Chamber events and workshops.
  • Exclusive invitations to VIP events and leadership roundtables.
  • Opportunity for a dedicated "Business Spotlight" on the Chamber homepage annually.
  • Priority for speaking engagements at Chamber events.
  • Personalized business advocacy and support.
  • Complimentary use of Chamber meeting rooms (limited hours).
